How it Works?

Draw a Picture

  • Use your finger to draw on the canvas
  • You can change the color of your drawing
  • Press the Undo button to undo the last drawing
  • Press the Delete button to delete the drawing

Listen to the Picture

  • Once you're done drawing
  • Hit the Play Button on the page
  • The results page will show you your drawing
  • The results will allow you to save your drawing
