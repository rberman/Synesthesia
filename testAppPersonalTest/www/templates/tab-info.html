How it Works?

Draw a Picture

  • Use your finger to draw a picture on the canvas.

  • You can change the pen color by clicking the paintbrush.

  • Click on the undo button to undo the last line you drew.

  • Click the new canvas button in the top left corner to start a new drawing.

Listen to a Drawing

  • Once you're done drawing, hit the blue Play button on the page in the top right corner.

  • The results page will show you your creation, and play music created from your drawing.

Save Your Creation

  • If you like your drawing, you can save it and come back to it. After you listen to your drawing, just click the Save button, and follow the instructions.

  • When naming your drawing, make sure you choose a new name that you haven't already used.

  • To open a saved drawing, open up a new drawing and click the Folder button. You can then select a saved drawing to continue working on or to play again.

  • Want to delete a saved drawing? Click the load button, and instead of hitting the drawing name, hit the trash button next to the drawing you want to delete.
